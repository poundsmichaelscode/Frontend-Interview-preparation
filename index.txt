 -=========HOW The Internet Works =============\


Understand what actually happens when you type a URL and hit enter. This mental model underpins everything you'll build.

Topics covered
DNS resolution: domain names → IP addresses
TCP/IP handshake and what 'connection' means
HTTP request/response cycle (methods, headers, status codes)
What a browser does with HTML: parsing → DOM → render
Client vs. server — who does what
